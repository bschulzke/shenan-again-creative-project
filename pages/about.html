What's a Tabletop Roleplaying Game?

Shenan-Again

Shenan-Again is a game of controlled chaos. You and your friends take on the role of a crew of thieves attempting to pull off a difficult heist using a Coil, an alien machine that warps time. You'll enjoy thrilling success, and face crushing—but often hilarious—failure.

In the end, Shenan-Again is a game about taking risks and learning from mistakes. It's about second (and third) chances. See, Shenan-Again isn't just a heist story. It's a time-loop heist story.

In Shenan-Again, you relive the same heist three times, giving you three chances to get it right. The second and third time around, you'll already know a lot of what's going on, letting you to perfect your plan.
