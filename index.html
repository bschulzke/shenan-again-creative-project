Shenan-Again

Shenan-Again is all about controlled chaos. You'll play as a crew of thieves pulling off a difficult heist, with a time-warping twist. You’ll enjoy thrilling successes, but you’ll also face crushing—yet often hilarious—failures. Shenan-Again is a game about taking risks and learning from mistakes. It’s about second chances.

This isn't just a heist story. It’s a time-loop heist story. In the game, you’ll relive the same heist three times, giving you the chance to reasses and perfect your plan after things inevitably falls apart the first time through.
